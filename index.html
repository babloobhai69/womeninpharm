Our Mission

To uplift, support, and amplify women in pharmacy by fostering inclusive education, impactful research, professional growth, and public health leadership.

About Women in Pharma

Women in Pharma is a collaborative platform dedicated to empowering women across pharmacy education, research, professional practice, and public health leadership. We bring together students, educators, researchers, and healthcare professionals to create opportunities for mentorship, learning, and innovation.

What We Do

Education & Capacity Building

Empowering women in pharmacy with knowledge, skills, mentorship, and professional development opportunities to excel in healthcare and public health.

Research & Policy Advocacy

Generating evidence, shaping informed policies, and amplifying the voices of women in pharmacy within healthcare and public health systems.

Leadership & Community Engagement

Supporting women in developing leadership confidence and contributing meaningfully to healthcare systems, public health initiatives, and community well-being.

Education & Capacity Building

Education and Capacity Building focuses on empowering women in pharmacy with knowledge, skills, and practical exposure needed to excel in healthcare and public health.

Through training programs, workshops, mentorship, and learning resources, we support women in strengthening academic foundations, professional competencies, and leadership abilities.

Public Health & Community Engagement

Public Health initiatives highlight the expanding role of pharmacy in addressing real-world healthcare challenges, vaccination programs, disease prevention, and community well-being.

We explore how pharmacy education connects with population health, public awareness, and community-based healthcare systems.

Research, Policy & Advocacy

Research and Advocacy focus on generating evidence, shaping informed policies, and strengthening gender equity within pharmacy education and healthcare systems.

Through research insights and policy discussions, we aim to build inclusive, evidence-based healthcare frameworks.

Read More →

Leadership & Professional Development

Leadership development supports women in pharmacy as they build and grow their academic and professional journeys across healthcare and public health.

Through mentorship-oriented content and expert guidance, we aim to strengthen leadership confidence and long-term professional growth.

Social Pharmacy

Social Pharmacy focuses on the intersection of medicines, public health systems, policy development, and evolving community healthcare needs.

It highlights pharmacy-led health initiatives, government programs, and the broader impact of pharmacists in disease prevention and health promotion.
