Education & Capacity Building

Education and Capacity Building is a central pillar of Women in Pharma – Education & Public Health. We focus on empowering women in pharmacy with the knowledge, skills, and practical exposure required to excel in healthcare systems, academic institutions, research environments, and public health practice.

Pharmacy education is continuously evolving in response to emerging healthcare challenges, technological advancements, and expanding public health responsibilities. Our initiative supports women learners and professionals in navigating this transformation with confidence, competence, and leadership clarity.

Our Approach

We aim to strengthen academic foundations while fostering professional growth through structured mentorship, experiential learning opportunities, and collaborative engagement. By connecting students and professionals with experienced educators, researchers, clinicians, and public health leaders, we help bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world healthcare practice.

Through carefully designed programs, we encourage critical thinking, ethical practice, interdisciplinary collaboration, and evidence-based decision-making — all essential qualities for effective pharmacy and public health leadership.

Focus Areas

  • Strengthening core pharmaceutical and public health knowledge
  • Developing clinical, research, and analytical competencies
  • Enhancing communication and leadership skills
  • Encouraging academic excellence and scholarly engagement
  • Promoting confidence and professional identity among women in pharmacy

Key Initiatives

  • National Mentorship Network for pharmacy students and early-career professionals
  • Career Development and Skill-Building Workshops
  • Leadership and Research Training Programs
  • Women Educators Development Initiatives
  • Academic Collaboration and Exchange Opportunities
  • Scholarship, Fellowship, and Research Support Programs

Why It Matters

Building strong educational foundations directly strengthens healthcare systems. By investing in women’s academic and professional growth, we contribute to improved patient care, ethical pharmaceutical practice, and better public health outcomes.

Education and Capacity Building not only enhances individual careers but also cultivates a generation of women leaders capable of shaping healthcare policy, advancing research, and driving innovation within pharmacy and public health ecosystems.
